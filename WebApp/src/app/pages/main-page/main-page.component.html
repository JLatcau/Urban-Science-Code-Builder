Help

You must provide this application with an input image. The input image should have the letters of the components structured in a 3 x 2 table format. Below is an example of what your image may look like. Please ensure you are writing on a blank white peice of paper.

This page provides you with two ways you can upload an image:

  • You are able to upload an image from your device by clicking the 'Upload From Device' button. This can be of file type png, jpg, and heic.
    You can also drag and drop an image from your file explorer. Simply hover the picture over the buttons.
  • You can also take a picture of a peice of paper using the camera of device by clicking the 'Take Picture' button.
